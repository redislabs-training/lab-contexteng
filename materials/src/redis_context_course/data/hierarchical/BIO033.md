# BIO033: Marine Ecology and Conservation

## Course Information

- **Department**: Biology
- **Credits**: 3
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: Tricia Valencia
- **Semester**: spring 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 69

## Description

Ocean ecosystems, biodiversity, and conservation strategies. This course examines marine habitats from coral reefs to deep sea vents, exploring species interactions, food webs, and human impacts. Students will analyze case studies of marine conservation efforts and develop proposals for protecting endangered marine ecosystems.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in marine ecology and conservation
- Implement marine ecology and conservation algorithms and techniques
- Apply marine ecology and conservation to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate marine ecology and conservation solutions
- Design and build complete biology systems
