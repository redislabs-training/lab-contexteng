# MATH024: Calculus I

## Course Information

- **Department**: Mathematics
- **Credits**: 4
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: Tommy Walter
- **Semester**: summer 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 68

## Description

Limits, derivatives, and introduction to integration. This course covers single-variable calculus, including limits, continuity, differentiation, and basic integration. Students will apply calculus to optimization problems, related rates, and area calculations.

## Prerequisites

- **MATH021**: Prerequisite for Calculus I (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in calculus i
- Implement calculus i algorithms and techniques
- Apply calculus i to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate calculus i solutions
- Design and build complete mathematics systems
