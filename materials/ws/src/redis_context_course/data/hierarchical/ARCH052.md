# ARCH052: Digital Fabrication

## Course Information

- **Department**: Architecture
- **Credits**: 3
- **Difficulty Level**: advanced
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: Danny Morgan
- **Semester**: winter 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 47

## Description

3D printing, CNC, and computational design. This course explores digital fabrication technologies and parametric design. Students will use Grasshopper, laser cutters, and 3D printers to create architectural prototypes.

## Prerequisites

- **ARCH042**: Prerequisite for Digital Fabrication (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in digital fabrication
- Implement digital fabrication algorithms and techniques
- Apply digital fabrication to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate digital fabrication solutions
- Design and build complete architecture systems
