# BIO036: Microbiology

## Course Information

- **Department**: Biology
- **Credits**: 4
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: Jeffrey Chavez
- **Semester**: winter 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 55

## Description

Bacteria, viruses, fungi, and their roles in health and disease. This course covers microbial structure, metabolism, genetics, and interactions with humans. Laboratory work includes culturing and identifying microorganisms.

## Prerequisites

- **BIO032**: Prerequisite for Microbiology (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in microbiology
- Implement microbiology algorithms and techniques
- Apply microbiology to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate microbiology solutions
- Design and build complete biology systems
