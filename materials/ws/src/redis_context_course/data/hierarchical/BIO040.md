# BIO040: Evolutionary Biology

## Course Information

- **Department**: Biology
- **Credits**: 3
- **Difficulty Level**: advanced
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: Brittany Farmer
- **Semester**: winter 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 33

## Description

Mechanisms of evolution and phylogenetics. This course examines natural selection, genetic drift, speciation, and the history of life on Earth. Students will analyze evolutionary patterns using molecular and morphological data.

## Prerequisites

- **BIO030**: Prerequisite for Evolutionary Biology (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in evolutionary biology
- Implement evolutionary biology algorithms and techniques
- Apply evolutionary biology to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate evolutionary biology solutions
- Design and build complete biology systems
