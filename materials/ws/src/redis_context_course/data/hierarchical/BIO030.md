# BIO030: Molecular Biology and Genetics

## Course Information

- **Department**: Biology
- **Credits**: 4
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: Anthony Rodriguez
- **Semester**: summer 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 57

## Description

DNA, RNA, protein synthesis, and genetic inheritance. This course explores the molecular basis of life, from DNA replication to gene expression. Students will learn about genetic mutations, inheritance patterns, and modern techniques like PCR and CRISPR. Laboratory sessions provide hands-on experience with molecular biology techniques.

## Prerequisites

- **BIO032**: Prerequisite for Molecular Biology and Genetics (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in molecular biology and genetics
- Implement molecular biology and genetics algorithms and techniques
- Apply molecular biology and genetics to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate molecular biology and genetics solutions
- Design and build complete biology systems
