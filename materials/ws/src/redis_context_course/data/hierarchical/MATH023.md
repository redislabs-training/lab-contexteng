## Course Information

- **Semester**: summer 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 69

## Description

Functions, trigonometry, and preparation for calculus. This course builds the algebraic and trigonometric foundations needed for success in calculus. Students will master functions, graphs, exponentials, logarithms, and trigonometric identities through problem-solving and applications.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in pre-calculus
- Implement pre-calculus algorithms and techniques
- Apply pre-calculus to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate pre-calculus solutions
- Design and build complete mathematics systems
