# BIO037: Ecology and Environmental Science

## Course Information

- **Department**: Biology
- **Credits**: 3
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: in_person
- **Instructor**: Sherri Baker
- **Semester**: fall 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 59

## Description

Ecosystems, populations, and environmental challenges. This course examines interactions between organisms and their environment, population dynamics, community ecology, and conservation biology.

## Prerequisites

- **BIO032**: Prerequisite for Ecology and Environmental Science (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in ecology and environmental science
- Implement ecology and environmental science algorithms and techniques
- Apply ecology and environmental science to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate ecology and environmental science solutions
- Design and build complete biology systems
