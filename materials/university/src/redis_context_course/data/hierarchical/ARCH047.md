# ARCH047: Urban Design and Planning

## Course Information

- **Department**: Architecture
- **Credits**: 3
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: online
- **Instructor**: John Pierce
- **Semester**: summer 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 34

## Description

City planning, public spaces, and urban development. This course examines urban form, zoning, transportation, and community design. Students will develop plans for urban neighborhoods and public spaces.

## Prerequisites

- **ARCH041**: Prerequisite for Urban Design and Planning (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in urban design and planning
- Implement urban design and planning algorithms and techniques
- Apply urban design and planning to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate urban design and planning solutions
- Design and build complete architecture systems
