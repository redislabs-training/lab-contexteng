# ARCH048: Sustainable Building Design

## Course Information

- **Department**: Architecture
- **Credits**: 4
- **Difficulty Level**: advanced
- **Format**: hybrid
- **Instructor**: Shane Henderson
- **Semester**: summer 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 69

## Description

Green architecture, energy efficiency, and LEED certification. This course teaches sustainable design principles for buildings that minimize environmental impact. Students will learn about passive solar design, green materials, water conservation, and renewable energy integration. Projects focus on designing net-zero energy buildings.

## Prerequisites

- **ARCH043**: Prerequisite for Sustainable Building Design (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in sustainable building design
- Implement sustainable building design algorithms and techniques
- Apply sustainable building design to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate sustainable building design solutions
- Design and build complete architecture systems
