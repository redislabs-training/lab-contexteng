# BIO035: Human Anatomy and Physiology

## Course Information

- **Department**: Biology
- **Credits**: 4
- **Difficulty Level**: intermediate
- **Format**: hybrid
- **Instructor**: Debra Davidson
- **Semester**: summer 2025
- **Max Enrollment**: 44

## Description

Structure and function of human body systems. This course examines the organization of the human body from cells to organ systems. Topics include skeletal, muscular, nervous, cardiovascular, and other major systems.

## Prerequisites

- **BIO032**: Prerequisite for Human Anatomy and Physiology (Minimum grade: C)

## Learning Objectives

- Understand core concepts in human anatomy and physiology
- Implement human anatomy and physiology algorithms and techniques
- Apply human anatomy and physiology to real-world problems
- Analyze and evaluate human anatomy and physiology solutions
- Design and build complete biology systems
